# Kawach (कवच) - The AI Safety Intelligence Ecosystem

**Built for the GDG & Rotary Club Bangalore Hackathon**  
**Theme**: Peacebuilding & Conflict Prevention

---

## 🛡️ Vision
Kawach (meaning "Shield" in Sanskrit) is a production-grade safety ecosystem designed to eliminate fear and prevent conflict. By leveraging **Google's most advanced AI models** and **Decentralized Networking**, Kawach ensures that no individual is ever truly alone, even in the most isolated "dark zones."

Our mission is to foster peace through immediate accountability, community resilience, and data-driven conflict prevention.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Unyielding SOS Pipeline
- **Multi-Modal Triggers**: SOS can be triggered via Native Hardware Interception (Power Button), Vigorous Shake (Accelerometer), or AI-driven Voice Detection ("Help Me", "SOS").
- **Lock-Screen Support**: Triggers work even when the phone is locked or asleep, bypassing OS-level latency.
- **Tactical Pause**: A 15-second haptic-feedback countdown to prevent false alarms.

### 2. Offline Resilience: BLE Mesh Relay
- **The Problem**: 70% of safety incidents happen in areas with poor or no internet.
- **The Solution**: Uses **Google Nearby Connections API** to create a decentralized mesh network.
- **Mechanism**: SOS signals "hop" from phone to phone using Bluetooth LE until they reach a node with an active internet connection.

### 3. Google Gemini Intelligence
- **Conflict Analysis**: Uses **Gemini Pro** to analyze the context of an SOS (audio, location, community data) and generate a detailed brief for responders.
- **Incident Summarization**: Automatically summarizes threats to help police coordinate the right type of peacebuilding response.

### 4. Community Safety Intelligence
- **Realtime Heatmap**: A live, community-synced map showing incident hotspots (Theft, Assault, Harassment).
- **PostGIS Integration**: Advanced geospatial queries to calculate dynamic "Safety Scores" for routes.
- **Behavioral AI**: On-device neural networks that detect anomalous patterns (e.g., sudden running followed by a fall).

### 5. Forensic Evidence Vault
- **Cryptographic Hashing**: All background audio/video evidence is hashed using **SHA-256** locally.
- **Immutable Record**: Hashes are sent to the Supabase cloud immediately, ensuring evidence is untamperable and legally admissible.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) (Mobile), [Vanilla JS](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript) (Web Dashboard).
- **AI/ML**: [Google Gemini Pro](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/), TensorFlow Lite (On-device audio/motion).
- **Backend**: [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) (Realtime DB, Storage, Edge Functions).
- **Geospatial**: [Google Maps Platform](https://mapsplatform.google.com/), PostGIS.
- **Communication**: Google Nearby Connections API, BLE.
